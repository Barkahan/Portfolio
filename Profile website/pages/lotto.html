Lottery Number Generator

A Practice project

I built a simple Lottery Number Generator as a way to practice my JavaScript and DOM manipulation skills. It randomly creates a new set of numbers at the click of a button, wrapped in a playful design that makes the experience feel light and fun. It’s a small project, but it helped me get more comfortable structuring functions, updating the UI dynamically, and building something that works end‑to‑end.

A simple number generator for practice.
