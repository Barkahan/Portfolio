About me

I started studying web development on Udemy over a year ago. Angela Yu’s Full‑Stack Bootcamp has taught me so much already. When I first started, I didn’t know what an HTML tag was or what CSS meant. Now I’m building small projects from scratch, and honestly, I’m amazed at how far I’ve come.

Learning this stuff hasn’t been easy, but that’s exactly why I enjoy it. I learn best by doing — building something, breaking it, and figuring out how to fix it. Every project I’ve made has pushed me a little further, whether it’s understanding how routes work in Express, getting layouts to behave in CSS, or just trusting my instincts more as I go. I’m still growing, still experimenting, and still improving my eye for design and structure.

But I’m proud of the progress I’ve made, and I’m excited to keep building things that show what I can do.
